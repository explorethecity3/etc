# Content Strategy for ExploreTheCity.in

A comprehensive guide to creating unique, valuable content for your website and Google AdSense approval.

## Why Content Matters

Google AdSense requires:
- **15-20 unique articles minimum** (30+ recommended)
- **High-quality, original content**
- **Regular updates**
- **Value for readers**

## Content Creation Process

### Step 1: Research

**Sources:**
- Visit destinations personally (best)
- Read other travel blogs (for inspiration, not copying)
- Watch YouTube travel videos
- Read official tourism websites
- Check TripAdvisor reviews for insights
- Join travel forums (India Mike, Lonely Planet)

**Tools:**
- Google Trends (find what people search)
- AnswerThePublic (find questions people ask)
- Keyword research tools (Ubersuggest, free)

### Step 2: Writing

**Quality Guidelines:**
1. **Original Content:** Never copy-paste
2. **Personal Touch:** Add your experiences
3. **Specific Details:** Names, addresses, prices
4. **Helpful Tips:** Practical information
5. **Structure:** Use headings, lists, short paragraphs
6. **Length:** 800-1500 words for blog posts

**Writing Template:**

```
Title: [Catchy, includes keyword]

Introduction (100-150 words)
- Hook the reader
- State what they'll learn
- Personal touch

Main Content (600-1000 words)
- Use H2 and H3 headings
- Break into sections
- Add lists and bullet points
- Include specific details

Practical Information
- Best time
- How to reach
- Where to stay
- Budget estimate

Conclusion (100-150 words)
- Summarize key points
- Call to action
- Encourage comments

```

### Step 3: Optimization

**SEO Checklist:**
- [ ] Keyword in title
- [ ] Keyword in first paragraph
- [ ] 2-3% keyword density
- [ ] Alt text for images
- [ ] Internal links to other pages
- [ ] External links to authoritative sites
- [ ] Meta description (150-160 characters)

**Example Keywords:**
- "[City name] travel guide"
- "Things to do in [City]"
- "[City] food guide"
- "Best time to visit [City]"
- "Budget travel [City]"

## Writing Tips

### Make It Unique

**Instead of:** "Mumbai is a beautiful city with many attractions."

**Write:** "Standing at Marine Drive at 6 AM, watching Mumbai wake up with the Arabian Sea breeze in your face, you'll understand why this city never sleeps. From the street vendors setting up chai stalls to early morning joggers, the energy is palpable."

### Add Personal Experience

**Generic:** "The Gateway of India is a popular monument."

**Personal:** "I visited the Gateway of India at sunset, and despite the crowds, managed to capture some stunning photos. Pro tip: Visit on a weekday morning before 8 AM for fewer tourists and better light."

### Be Specific

**Vague:** "There are many good restaurants."

**Specific:** "Try Karim's near Jama Masjid (established 1913) for authentic Mughlai food. Their mutton seekh kebab (‚Çπ280) and roomali roti (‚Çπ30) are legendary. Expect to wait 20-30 minutes during lunch hours."

### Use Conversational Tone

**Formal:** "One should visit the aforementioned locations."

**Conversational:** "Trust me, you don't want to miss these spots! I've been to Delhi five times, and these places never disappoint."

## Tracking Success

### Metrics to Monitor

**Content Performance:**
- Page views per article
- Time on page (target: 2+ minutes)
- Bounce rate (target: under 70%)
- Social shares

**SEO Performance:**
- Organic traffic
- Keyword rankings
- Click-through rate
- Backlinks

**AdSense Performance:**
- Page RPM (revenue per 1000 views)
- CTR (click-through rate)
- CPC (cost per click)

### Monthly Review

Questions to ask:
1. Which articles got most traffic?
2. What topics performed best?
3. Which keywords are ranking?
4. What's the average time on site?
5. How many new posts published?
6. How many posts updated?
